THE SME PACKAGES

The perception that individuals have of any business will definitely play a key role in how many actual customers the business can attract and retain in the market place. The effective branding of a business directly impacts the opinion that the market will have of such a business.

In Nigeria today, a lot of SMEs are facing the challenge of effective branding which will directly affect their competitiveness in the market space. MariePhilip Consulting seeks to address this by providing cost effective and efficient branding services to these businesses. Our goal is to provide our world class strategy and branding services to SMEs in Nigeria at a fraction of the cost.

After carefully examining the critical needs of enterprises, we have effectively broken the services into four (4) unique packages for businesses in different categories ranging from Micro to Small and Medium Enterprises.

BASIC PACKAGE

Our Basic Package is designed for Mirco to Small start-up businesses that need the immediate brand identity to get business off the ground.
